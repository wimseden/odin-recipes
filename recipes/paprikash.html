Instant Pot Chicken Paprikash

Chicken Paprikash contains paprika and noodles.

Ingredients
  • 12 ounce package egg noodles
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp minced aromatics
  • 1.5 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 shallots, thinly sliced
  • 6 cloves garlic, coarsely chopped
  • 2 cups arrabiatta pasta sauce
  • 1/4 cup chicken broth
  • 3 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 2 pounds boneless skinless chicken thighs
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 3 tbsp paprika
  1. Fill large pot with salted water and bring to rapid boil. Boil egg noodles 7 to 9 minutes. Drain and toss with butter, aromatics, salt and pepper.
  2. Set instant pot to Saute. Add oil, shallots, garlic; saute 2-3 minutes. Add pasta sauce, chicken broth, vinegar.
  3. Season chicken with salt and pepper. Add to instant pot, set high pressure and timer for 12 minutes.
  4. Natural release pressure.
  5. Place 1/2 cup yogurt in a small bowl. Add 1/2 cup pasta sauce, 1 tbsp at a time to temper the yogurt. Add paprika and blend. Pour yogurt mixture into the pot.
  6. Shred chicken, serve over noodles. Serve with yogurt and aromatic herbs.

You can also substitute sour cream for the yogurt.
