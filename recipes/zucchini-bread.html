Delicious Lemony Zucchini Bread

Ingredients
  • 1.5 cups shredded zucchini
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1.5 cup flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • zest of 1 lemon
  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees. Grease 8x4 loaf pan.
  2. In a bowl, combine zucchini, egg, oil amd lemon zest.
  3. In another bowl, sift together flour, salt, baking soda, baking powder and cinnamon.
  4. Combine the bowls into a batter and pour into prepared pan.
  5. Bake 45 minutes or until a knife in the center comes out clean. Cool for 10 minutes before serving.
