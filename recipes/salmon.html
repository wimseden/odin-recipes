Baked Salmon

delicious baked salmon

Ingredients
  • Cooking Spray or oil
  • 2 pound salmon fillet
  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 2 tbsp seafood seasoning (such as Old Bay)

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350. Oil or spray a large baking sheet.
  2. Place salmon on baking sheet. Spread with mayonnaise and coat with seasoning
  3. Bake for 20 minutes until fish flakes easily with a fork.
